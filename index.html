Revolutionizing Grayscale: Deep Learning Model Brings Old Photos to Life!

By Parth Lathiya | AI/ML, Deep Learning | Mar 2025 - Apr 2025

SURAT – A cutting-edge, deep learning-based image colorization tool, leveraging U-Net architecture and the LAB color space, has been developed, capable of transforming grayscale inputs into realistic colorized outputs with high fidelity at $256\times256$ resolution.

This individual project focused on creating a robust system for generating colorized images from grayscale inputs. It involved preprocessing extensive datasets, including splitting them into training, validation, and test sets to ensure comprehensive model training.

The core of the solution lies in a U-Net architecture, which utilized the LAB color space to predict the 'ab' channels, subsequently combining them with the original 'L' channel for realistic outputs. The image colorization pipeline encompassed both training and inference workflows, supporting both single and batch image processing.

Model training was designed and executed using PyTorch, with results displayed alongside original images for direct comparison and analysis. A significant achievement was the model's ability to generalize by processing approximately 118,000 diverse grayscale images, highlighting its potential for automatic image restoration and enhancement tasks.
